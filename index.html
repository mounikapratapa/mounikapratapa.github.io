About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate specializing in cryptography and privacy-enhancing technologies. With over 5 years of research experience, I focus on developing secure multiparty computations and homomorphic encryption schemes. My passion lies in applying these technologies to real-world challenges, particularly in privacy-preserving data analysis.
